Chicken Tacos

These chicken tacos are a delicious and easy meal option. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with taco seasoning.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing the seasoned chicken on each tortilla.
  4. Top with diced tomatoes, lettuce, cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
