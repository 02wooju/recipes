Pasta Primavera

This Pasta Primavera is a vibrant and fresh dish that is easy to make and sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes, zucchini, bell pepper, and broccoli. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Toss in the cooked pasta and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
