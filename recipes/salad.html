Caesar Salad

This Caesar Salad is a classic dish with crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, and crunchy croutons. Perfect as a side or a light meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: lemon wedges for serving

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and Caesar dressing. Toss until the lettuce is well coated.
  2. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and croutons. Toss gently to combine.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately, garnished with lemon wedges if desired.
